Cancellation and Payment Timing

Short stays (29 nights or fewer): Free cancellation until 48 hours before check-in (4pm local time). Credit card on file to guarantee reservation and charged 48 hours prior to arrival. Minimum length of stay requirements may vary. Completion of guest registration portal is required.
Extended stays (30 nights or longer): Cancellation must occur no fewer than 28 days prior to check-in. Credit card payment for thirty percent (30%) of the reservation is collected upon booking. The remaining balance shall be collected seven (7) days prior to arrival. If reservation is confirmed within a week of check-in, full payment is due upon booking confirmation. Completion of guest registration portal is required within 48 hours of booking.

When you book on other sites, you’ll likely encounter stricter cancellation policies. Always make sure to check a site’s cancellation policy before booking and reach out to them directly if you have questions.
